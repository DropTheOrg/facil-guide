## Step 1: Open Settings

Tap **Settings** on your home screen.

## Step 2: Find the email section

**On iPhone:** Tap **Mail**, then **Accounts**, then **Add Account**.

**On Android:** Tap **Accounts** (or **Passwords & Accounts**), then **Add Account**, then **Email** or your email provider's name.

## Step 3: Choose your email provider

You'll see a list of providers: Google (Gmail), Microsoft (Outlook, Hotmail), Yahoo, and others. **Tap the one that matches your email**. If yours isn't listed, tap **Other**.

## Step 4: Enter your email and password

Type your full email address (for example, yourname@gmail.com) and tap **Next**. Then type your password and tap **Sign In**. Your phone will verify the account.

## Step 5: Choose what to sync

You'll be asked what to sync: Mail, Contacts, Calendars. At minimum, turn on **Mail**. Tap **Save** or **Done**. Your email will now appear in the Mail app (iPhone) or Gmail app (Android).
