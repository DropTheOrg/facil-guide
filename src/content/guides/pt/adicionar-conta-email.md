## Passo 1: Open Definicoes

Tap **Definicoes** on your home screen.

## Passo 2: Find the email section

**No iPhone:** Tap **Mail**, then **Accounts**, then **Add Account**.

**No Android:** Tap **Accounts** (or **Passwords & Accounts**), then **Add Account**, then **Email** or your email provider's name.

## Passo 3: Escolha your email provider

You'll see a list of providers: Google (Gmail), Microsoft (Outlook, Hotmail), Yahoo, and others. **Tap the one that matches your email**. If yours isn't listed, tap **Other**.

## Passo 4: Enter your email and password

Type your full email address (for example, yourname@gmail.com) and tap **Next**. Then type your password and tap **Sign In**. O seu telefone will verify the account.

## Passo 5: Escolha what to sync

You'll be asked what to sync: Mail, Contacts, Calendars. At minimum, turn on **Mail**. Tap **Save** or **Done**. Your email will now appear in the Mail app (iPhone) or Gmail app (Android).
