## Step 1: Open Settings

Tap **Settings** on your home screen.

## Step 2: Find the ringtone setting

**On iPhone:** Tap **Sounds & Haptics**, then tap **Ringtone**.

**On Android:** Tap **Sound** (or **Sound & Vibration**), then tap **Phone ringtone**.

## Step 3: Choose a new ringtone

You'll see a list of ringtone options. **Tap any name** to hear a preview. When you find one you like, it will be selected automatically (iPhone) or tap **OK/Save** (Android). Your new ringtone is now set.
