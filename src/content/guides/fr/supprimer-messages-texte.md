## Etape 1: Open the Messages app

Tap the **Messages** app on your home screen. It's the green icon with a white speech bubble (iPhone) or a blue icon (Android).

## Etape 2: Supprimer a single message

Open a conversation. **Press and hold** the specific message you want to delete. A menu will appear. Tap **Supprimer** or the trash icon. Confirm by tapping **Supprimer** again.

## Etape 3: Supprimer an entire conversation

Go back to the main Messages list. **Swipe left** on the conversation you want to delete (iPhone) or **press and hold** the conversation (Android). Tap **Supprimer**. The entire conversation will be removed.

## Etape 4: Supprimer multiple conversations

Tap **Edit** at the top (iPhone) or the three dots menu (Android). Selectionnez the conversations you want to remove by tapping the circles next to them. Then tap **Supprimer** to remove them all at once.
