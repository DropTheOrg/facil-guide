## Ce dont vous aurez besoin

- Votre telephone iPhone ou Android

## Etape 1: Open Quick Reglages

- **Sur iPhone :** Swipe down from the **top-right corner** of the screen
- **Sur Android :** Swipe down from the **top** of the screen with two fingers

## Etape 2: Tap Do Not Disturb

- **Sur iPhone :** Tap **Focus**, then tap **Do Not Disturb** (crescent moon icon)
- **Sur Android :** Look for the **Do Not Disturb** tile (circle with a line through it) and tap it

The icon will light up, meaning Do Not Disturb is now active.

Votre telephone will stop ringing, vibrating, and showing notification banners.

## Etape 3: Set a Schedule (Optional)

If you want Do Not Disturb to turn on and off automatically:

- **Sur iPhone :** Go to **Reglages** > **Focus** > **Do Not Disturb** > **Add Schedule**. Set it to turn on at bedtime and off in the morning.
- **Sur Android :** Go to **Reglages** > **Sound** > **Do Not Disturb** > **Schedules**. Set your sleep hours.

For example, set it from 10 PM to 7 AM every night.

## Etape 4: Turn It Off

When you are ready to receive notifications again:

1. Open Quick Reglages (same swipe as Step 1)
2. Tap the Do Not Disturb icon again to turn it off

The icon will no longer be highlighted, and notifications will resume.

## Vous avez reussi !

You can now silence your phone whenever you need peace and quiet. Use it at night, during meals, in meetings, or anytime you want to focus without interruptions.
