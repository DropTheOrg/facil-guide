## What You Will Need

- Your iPhone or Android phone
- A web browser (Safari, Chrome, or Firefox)

## Step 1: Go to the Website You Want to Save

Open your browser and navigate to the website you want to bookmark. For example, go to your favorite news site or recipe page.

Make sure you are on the exact page you want to save.

## Step 2: Tap the Share or Menu Button

- **On iPhone (Safari):** Tap the **Share** button at the bottom (square with an arrow pointing up)
- **On Android (Chrome):** Tap the **three dots** in the top-right corner

## Step 3: Add the Bookmark

- **On iPhone (Safari):** Tap **Add Bookmark**. You can change the name and choose a folder. Tap **Save**.
- **On Android (Chrome):** Tap the **star** icon at the top. The page is bookmarked instantly.

The website is now saved.

## Step 4: Find Your Bookmarks Later

To return to a bookmarked website:

- **On iPhone (Safari):** Tap the **book icon** at the bottom of the screen. Your bookmarks are listed there.
- **On Android (Chrome):** Tap the **three dots** > **Bookmarks**. Or type in the address bar and your bookmarks will appear in suggestions.

Tap any bookmark to open that website instantly.

**Bonus tip:** To add a website to your **home screen** as an icon:
- **On iPhone:** Share button > **Add to Home Screen**
- **On Android:** Three dots > **Add to Home screen**

## You Did It!

You can now save any website and return to it with one tap. No more trying to remember website addresses or searching for the same page again.
