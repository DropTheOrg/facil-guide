## Step 1: Open Settings

Tap **Settings** on your home screen.

## Step 2: Turn on Wi-Fi

**On iPhone:** Tap **Wi-Fi** and make sure the switch at the top is turned **on** (green).

**On Android:** Tap **Network & Internet** (or **Connections**), then tap **Wi-Fi** and turn the switch **on**.

## Step 3: Choose a network

Your phone will show a list of nearby Wi-Fi networks. **Tap the name** of the network you want to join. If it has a lock icon, you'll need a password.

## Step 4: Enter the password

Type the Wi-Fi password and tap **Join** (iPhone) or **Connect** (Android). Once connected, you'll see a Wi-Fi icon at the top of your screen. You're now online.
