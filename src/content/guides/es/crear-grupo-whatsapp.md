## What You Will Need

- Your iPhone or Android phone
- WhatsApp installed
- The people you want to add must be in your Contacts and have WhatsApp

## Step 1: Open WhatsApp

Tap the **WhatsApp** icon to open the app.

## Step 2: Start a New Group

- **On iPhone:** Tap **Chats** at the bottom, then tap **New Group** (or tap the pencil icon and then New Group)
- **On Android:** Tap the **three dots** in the top-right corner, then tap **New Group**

## Step 3: Add Members

You will see your contacts list. Tap the names of the people you want to add to the group.

A blue checkmark will appear next to each selected person. You can add as many people as you like.

When you have selected everyone, tap **Next** (arrow icon).

## Step 4: Name Your Group

Type a name for your group. Choose something clear, like:

- "Family Chat"
- "Book Club"
- "Building Committee"
- "Weekend Plans"

You can also add a **group photo** by tapping the camera icon. This is optional.

Tap **Create** or the **checkmark** to finish.

## Step 5: Send Your First Message

Your group is created. You will see all members listed.

Type your first message in the text box and tap **Send**. Everyone in the group will receive it.

**Useful group features:**
- **Reply to a specific message:** Press and hold a message, then tap Reply
- **Pin important messages:** Press and hold, tap Pin (useful for addresses or dates)
- **Share photos and documents:** Tap the + or paperclip icon

## You Did It!

You created a WhatsApp group. Everyone in the group can see and reply to messages. This is perfect for coordinating with family, planning events, or keeping in touch with friends.
