## Paso 1: Open the app switcher

**On iPhone (no Home button):** Swipe up from the bottom of the screen and pause in the middle.

**On iPhone (with Home button):** Double-press the Home button.

**En Android:** Tap the square button at the bottom of the screen, or swipe up and hold.

## Paso 2: Find the app you want to close

You'll see cards showing all your open apps. **Swipe left or right** to find the app you want to close.

## Paso 3: Close the app

**Swipe the app card up** and off the top of the screen. The app is now closed. Repeat for any other apps you want to close.
